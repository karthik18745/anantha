History of the Place

Ananthagiri Hills, located in the Vikarabad district of Telangana, is known for its lush greenery and pleasant climate. The hills are named after the Anantha Padmanabha Swamy Temple, which is believed to be ancient. The area has historical significance due to its connections with the Kakatiya dynasty and its strategic importance during various historical periods. The region has also been a part of several dynastic rule changes and is known for its scenic beauty and as a weekend getaway from Hyderabad.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Ananthagiri Hills is from October to March. During these months, the weather is cool and pleasant, ideal for trekking and enjoying the natural beauty of the hills.

Nearby Places to Visit
  • Rao Potharaju Temple: A historical temple dedicated to the local deity, known for its serene surroundings and historical significance.
  • Nagadhira Reservoir: A beautiful reservoir offering panoramic views and a tranquil environment.
  • Kali Temple: A temple located amidst the hills, known for its scenic location and spiritual significance.
  • Vikarabad: The nearby town with attractions like the Bhongir Fort and scenic beauty.
  • Hyderabad: The city is about 80 km away, offering various historical and cultural attractions.
Travel Routes

To reach Ananthagiri Hills, you can take the following routes:

  • From Hyderabad Airport: Take a cab or bus to Vikarabad, approximately 80 km away, and then proceed to Ananthagiri Hills.
  • From Secunderabad Railway Station: Ananthagiri Hills is about 80 km away; you can hire a cab or take a bus to Vikarabad and then to the hills.
  • From Hyderabad Bus Stand: Buses and cabs are available to Vikarabad, from where you can reach Ananthagiri Hills.
